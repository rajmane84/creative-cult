# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Creatives (photographers, motion designers, illustrators) looking for client projects, and clients looking to hire them. The creatives need to showcase their portfolio, skills, and experience effectively.

## Product Purpose

To connect clients with talented creatives. The creative onboarding ensures that we collect high-quality, structured data about their skills, experience, and portfolio so clients can easily find and evaluate them.

## Positioning

A premium, elegant, and frictionless platform for top-tier creatives to find high-quality work without the clutter of typical freelance marketplaces.

## Brand Personality

Sleek, modern, elegant, professional, tasteful, unobtrusive.

## Anti-references

- Cluttered, overwhelming forms (like typical enterprise software).
- Overly playful or chaotic designs.
- Generic SaaS templates with too many borders and boxes.

## Design Principles

- frictionless onboarding
- progressive disclosure (don't overwhelm with too many fields at once)
- elegant micro-interactions to provide subtle feedback
- typography-driven hierarchy

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Ensure forms are fully accessible via keyboard. High contrast for placeholder and helper text.
